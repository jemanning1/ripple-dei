About Ripple

What is Ripple?

Ripple is a collaborative DEI (Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion) knowledge hub created by and for the Irish Public Service and Semi-State Collaboration Group.

We believe DEI knowledge should be:

  • Accessible - Available on any device, searchable, easy to find
  • Practical - Real-world guidance for everyday situations
  • Collaborative - Shared wisdom across organisations
  • Irish-focused - Relevant to our context, laws, and culture

Submissions go to the collaborative group for review. Approved resources typically appear on the site within 1-2 weeks. You'll be credited for your contribution (or can remain anonymous if you prefer).

Who's Behind This?

Ripple is a collaborative project of the Irish Public Service and Semi-State Collaboration Group - DEI practitioners working together to share knowledge and best practices across organisations.
