RIPPLE DEI KNOWLEDGE HUB

Ripple is a collaborative DEI (Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion) knowledge hub developed by and for Ireland's semi-state and public sector organisations.

This platform is designed to:
- Share practical DEI resources across organisations
- Answer everyday workplace DEI questions
- Build a collaborative, peer-reviewed knowledge base
- Support public sector workers with accessible, mobile-first tools
